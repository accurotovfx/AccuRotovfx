About AccuRoto VFX

AccuRoto VFX is a Bangalore-based VFX studio founded in 2025, focused on delivering cinematic-quality rotoscoping for feature films and OTT platforms. With a dedicated team of skilled artists, we ensure precision, consistency, and on-time delivery.

While rotoscoping is our current core service, we are continuously evolving and preparing to expand into matchmove, paint, clean-up, wire removal, and chroma services as projects grow. We welcome both small and large-scale roto work.
